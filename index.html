Demographic Info

Roza is a biological researcher who has recently been working on a project to synthesize proteins that taste like beef. She believes that bacteria from people's belly buttons is the key to find the perfect bacteria sample that is missing in the current formula to synthesize the beef. Partnering with Improbable Beef she has taken samples from the navels/belly buttons of people across the country and has kept track of each individual person with an ID assigned to each bacterial sample.
Based on the Subject ID:

  • The bar chart shows the top 10 bacterial species i.e. Operational Taxonomic Unit(OTU ID) found in each subject's belly buttons.
  • The gauge chart shows weekly washing frquency of their belly button.
  • The bubble chart, shows bacterial cultures per sample i.e. corresponding sample values(bacterial species name for each OTU ID) against the OTU ID.
